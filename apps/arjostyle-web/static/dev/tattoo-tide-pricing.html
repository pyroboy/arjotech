Frequently Asked Questions

Yes! We support CSV import from Excel, Google Sheets, or any other booking system. Our team can also help with migration.

Do I need technical skills to use Tattoo Tide?

Not at all. If you can use Instagram, you can use Tattoo Tide. We handle all the technical stuff. Setup takes less than 10 minutes.

No contracts. Cancel anytime. We also offer a 14-day free trial on all plans so you can try before you buy.

Can I use my own domain?

Absolutely! Professional and Studio plans support custom domains (yourstudio.com). We'll help you set it up.

What payment methods can I accept?

We integrate with GCash, Maya, bank transfers, credit cards, and PayPal. You can accept deposits and full payments.
